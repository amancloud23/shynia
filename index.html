Our Story

Beauty Rooted in
Nature & Science

Shynia was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves to feel radiant in their own skin. Founded in 2020 by beauty enthusiast and biochemist Shynia Kapoor, our brand blends ancient botanical wisdom with cutting-edge dermatological research.

Every product is hand-crafted in small batches, free from harmful chemicals, and wrapped in sustainable packaging — because we care about your skin and our planet.
